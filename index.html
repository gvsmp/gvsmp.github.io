As soon as you join, you can immediately start punching a tree and play Minecraft how it was meant to be played. There are no claims, limits, warps, or anything that can limit you or your creativity. We welcome players of all styles, from PVPers, builders, redstone engineers, and everyone in between. So come and join us on Good Vibes SMP at play.gvsmp.net!
